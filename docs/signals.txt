Signals in ``brabeion``
=======================

``brabeion`` makes one signal available to developers, which lives at
``brabeion.signals``.

``badge_awarded``
-----------------

This signal is sent whenever a badge is awarded to a user.  It provides a
single argument, ``badge``, which is an instance of
``brabeion.models.BadgeAwarded``, and makes available  ``user``, ``badge``, and
``awarded_at`` time attributes.
